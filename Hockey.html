Hockey

Hockey is a team sport in which two teams play against each other by trying to maneuver a ball or a puck into the opponent's goal using a hockey stick. It is played on grass, artificial turf, ice, or field.

Rules of Hockey

  • Each team consists of 11 players, including a goalkeeper.
  • The game is played in two halves, each lasting 35 minutes.
  • Players can use their sticks to hit the ball but cannot use any part of their body.
  • A goal is scored when the ball is hit into the opponent's net.
  • Fouls can result in free hits or penalty corners for the opposing team.
  • The team with the most goals at the end of the game wins.

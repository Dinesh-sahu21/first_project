Table Tennis

Table Tennis, also known as Ping Pong, is a fast-paced indoor sport played by two or four players. Players use small paddles to hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table divided by a net.

Rules of Table Tennis

  • Each match is played in a best-of-five or best-of-seven format.
  • Players serve the ball diagonally across the table.
  • The ball must bounce once on each side before being returned.
  • A point is scored when the opponent fails to return the ball correctly.
  • The first player to reach 11 points wins the game, but must win by at least 2 points.
